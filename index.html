About Xiamen Agro Limited

Xiamen Agro Limited specializes in key aspects of the agricultural value chain, with a significant focus on **aggregation**. We are involved in the **procurement, packaging, and processing** of agricultural produce. Our core strength lies in **distribution**, supplying both processed produce and raw food materials. We are proud trialers of commodities like beans and rice to institutions including schools and universities.

Our operations cover a nationwide reach within Nigeria, with key addresses in **Nasarawa, Lagos (Headquarters), and Abuja**. While our primary market is **domestic distribution** within Nigeria, we also handle distribution to **Ghana**. Our business is focused on **produce, not animal products**. We are committed to building trust and comfort with our clients and partners through reliable practices and quality produce.
