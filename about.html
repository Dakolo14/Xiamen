About Us

Home / About Us

Rooted in the Soil. Driven by Purpose.

Founded to transform the agricultural value chain, Xiamen Foods & Agro Limited connects Nigerian farmers to profitable markets through efficient aggregation, processing, and nationwide distribution.

With regional offices in Nasarawa, Benue, and Ogun, our proximity to Nigeria's most fertile farmlands allows us to source fresh, organic, and high-quality produce directly from farming communities.

Our work supports smallholder farmers with fair prices, market access, and training — while delivering consistent, safe, and traceable products to consumers and institutions.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted agricultural brand — feeding nations and empowering farmers.

Our Mission

To deliver premium-quality agro-produce through sustainable sourcing, innovation, and strong distribution networks.
